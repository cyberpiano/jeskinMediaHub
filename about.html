Our Identity

Beyond The Ink

Established in 2019 to redefine professional branding and high-end printing across the Ashanti Region & Beyond.

Our Journey

Jeskin Media Hub (formerly CandiTek Enterprise) began with a clear mission: to solve the reliability gap in the printing market. We started as a small creative homebased force and have grown into a preferred partner for educational and corporate institutions.

Based in the heart of Kumasi, we combine local craftsmanship with world-class printing technology. We don't just deliver products; we build brand legacies.

Our Mission Statement

"To empower businesses by delivering superior branding and advertising solutions that amplify their voice and drive real growth."

Proven Track Record
